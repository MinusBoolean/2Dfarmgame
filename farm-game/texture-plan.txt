SUMMARY
========================================================================

Total distinct texture keys: 36

Breakdown by scene:
  FarmScene:           19 textures (10 base tiles + 4 state overlays + 3 paths + 1 tree trunk + 1 night)
  MineScene:           13 textures (mine tiles, ore types, stairs, collectibles)
  PondScene:            2 textures (water, dock) + reuses tile_grass from FarmScene
  GreenhouseScene:      7 textures (wall, interior, soil, and 4 tile states)

Shared/duplicate colors across scenes (potential texture reuse):
  #4a8c3f  tile_grass       -> FarmScene + PondScene
  #8b6914  farm_soil/path_wood/gh_soil -> multiple contexts, same hex
  #808080  stone_fence/path_stone -> FarmScene (fence and path share color)
  #8B4513  workshop/pond_dock/tree_trunk -> FarmScene + PondScene
  #2d8a2d  overlay_mature/gh_mature -> FarmScene + GreenhouseScene
  #606060  overlay_dead/gh_dead -> FarmScene + GreenhouseScene
  #88ccff  greenhouse_entrance/gh_wall -> FarmScene entrance + GreenhouseScene wall

Priority recommendation:
  HIGH   - Mine tiles (13 textures, most complex scene)
  HIGH   - Farm base tiles (10 textures, largest visible area)
  MEDIUM - Farm tile state overlays (4 textures, frequently updated)
  MEDIUM - Greenhouse tiles (7 textures, dedicated scene)
  MEDIUM - Paths (3 textures, player-placed)
  LOW    - Pond tiles (2 textures, small scene)
  LOW    - Tree trunk (1 texture, simple element)
  LOW    - Night overlay (1 texture, single overlay rectangle)
